Installing R and RStudio

You will need both R and RStudio for this course. R is a statistical computing and graphic programming language, and RStudio is the IDE for R that we will be using. Installing these on your laptop before our first meeting will help us move along at a quicker pace.

To install R, follow the instructions for your OS here: https://cran.rstudio.com/. To install RStudio, download the FREE version here: https://www.rstudio.com/products/rstudio/download/.
